FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about booking and visiting us.

How do I cancel or reschedule? ▼
You can cancel or reschedule up to 3 hours before your appointment. Use the confirmation number from your email or call us at +91 98765 00001.
Is there a cancellation fee? ▼
Cancellations made 3+ hours in advance are free. No-shows or late cancellations may incur a 20% service fee.
Do you accept walk-ins? ▼
Yes, we do accept walk-ins based on availability. However, we strongly recommend booking in advance to avoid waiting.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept Cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit/Debit Cards, and Net Banking.
Will I get a reminder before my appointment? ▼
Yes! We send an email and SMS reminder 24 hours before your appointment. Make sure to enter a valid phone and email during booking.
